Draft protocol is protocol which has not yet been checked over and accepted as the best way of doing things. It's here for comment and discussion until it's complete.

When ready for final review, draft protocol should be moved to review protocol, by replacing {{Draft protocol}} with {{Review protocol}}, as described in the protocol-writing protocol.
