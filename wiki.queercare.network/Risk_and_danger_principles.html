These principles describe how to manage risk and danger

Don't create another patient

If you become seriously injured, espeically if it is an injury that prevents you functioning, while doing care, the situation is now worse than it was before you intervened- Your own injury or illness must be solved as well as the casualties.

It is better to not intervene than to create another patient.
