These are the queercare resources for how to do care safely - you can read about the logic and assumptions behind them, the overriding policy that flows from that.

This policy, and our knowledge and experience gives the basis for protocols for dropping off and picking up items from houses in self isolation, disinfecting things and organising your group chat

If your group is organising using these protocols, please add yourself to the form here

All the resources are below!
